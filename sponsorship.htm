You can donate to the band for free! All you need to do is register at www.easyfundraising.org.uk. You donate to the band by accessing your usual online shopping sites through the easyfundraising site. Please give it a try!

Most importantly, please come and support our concerts!

Other ways to support the band

Each member of the band pays a monthly fee of around £15 and we then have to do at least one concert a month to cover the day to day costs of running the band. Any extra money goes on new music, stands, instruments and putting on concerts for the good of the community as a whole.

There are many ways you can help the band. You can book the band for a Concert, buy advertising space in our concert programmes, or sponsor a Concert Series. If you wish to help support the band, please email manager@cambridgeband.co.uk.
